Paradise Nursery Project Requirements
======================================

General Overview:
----------------
Create a "Paradise Nursery" shopping application interface allowing users to browse houseplants and manage a shopping cart.

Key Pages & Features:
--------------------
1. Landing Page:
   - Background image implemented via CSS.
   - Company name and "About Us" paragraph.
   - "Get Started" button linked to the product listing.

2. Product Listing Page:
   - At least 6 plants per category.
   - 3 categories (Air Purifying, Aromatic, Low Maintenance).
   - Each plant: thumbnail, name, price, description.
   - "Add to Cart" button (disables after selection).
   - Dynamic Navbar with cart quantity icon.

3. Shopping Cart Page:
   - List of items with thumbnail, name, unit price, and subtotal.
   - Total number of items and total cost of all items.
   - Increment/Decrement buttons to adjust quantities.
   - Delete button to remove items.
   - "Continue Shopping" button.
   - "Checkout" button displaying a "Coming Soon" message.

Evaluation Requirements:
-----------------------
- Author: Brian McCarthy must be on all files.
- Redux implementation for cart logic.
- Responsive design with polished UI.
- No AI Studio branding or mentions in the final UI.
