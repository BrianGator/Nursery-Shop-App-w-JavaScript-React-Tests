E-PlantShopping Project Requirements
======================================

General Overview:
----------------
Create an "E-PlantShopping" application interface allowing users to browse houseplants and manage a shopping cart.

Key Pages & Features:
--------------------
1. Landing Page:
   - Background image implemented via CSS.
   - Company name "Welcome to E-PlantShopping" prominently displayed.
   - "Get Started" button linked to the product listing.

2. Product Listing Page:
   - Categorized plants (Fruit Plants, Flower Plants, etc.).
   - "Add to Cart" functional with button disabling.
   - Navbar with live quantity counter.

3. Shopping Cart Page:
   - Subtotals per item type and absolute Grand Total.
   - Quantity controls and item removal.
   - Checkout button with "Coming Soon" alert.
